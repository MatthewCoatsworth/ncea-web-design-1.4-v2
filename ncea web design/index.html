Why I use Xd

Adobe experience design or Adobe Xd is a vector-based editing app designed for creating user interfaces and experiences to make this easy and intuitive it has many handy features suitable for me such as the ability to repeat shapes and groups with 2 clicks and being able to create a master component which when changed will impact all secondary components meaning that I can make UI's very quickly and effectively it is part of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite of apps although it can also be brought individually.
